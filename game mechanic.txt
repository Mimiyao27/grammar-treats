GAME MECHANICS

1. Scoring and Treat Collection

Players earn points for performance and treats as a progression currency.
Points determine leaderboard ranking, while treats are used to unlock new levels.

Per Question Rewards:

Correct Answer: +10 points, +2 treats

Incorrect Answer: +2 points, 0 treats



---

2. Score Saving and Progress Tracking

After completing each level, players are required to manually save their score to record their progress.

Only saved scores will:

Count toward the leaderboard

Be included in total points and treats

Be used for unlocking the next level


Unsaved progress will not be recorded, encouraging responsibility and intentional progression.



---

3. Level Structure and Unlocking

The game consists of five levels, each focusing on a specific grammar skill:

Level 1: Capitalization

Level 2: Punctuation

Level 3: Sentence Structure

Level 4: Verb Usage

Level 5: Comprehension


Starting Point:

Only Level 1 is unlocked at the beginning.


Level Format:

Each level contains 10 questions

Maximum per level: 100 points and 20 treats


Treat Requirements to Unlock Levels:

Level 1 - 21 treats to Unlock Level 2 
Level 2 - 36 total treats to Unlock Level 3
Level 3 - 111 total treats to Unlock Level 4
Level 4 - 186 total treats to Unlock Level 5


Note:
Treats are cumulative. Players who do not meet the required number must replay previous levels to earn more treats, ensuring mastery before progression.


---

4. Performance Rankings

The system tracks and encourages competitive and consistent performance.

Ranking Criteria:

Total points earned

Total treats collected

Accuracy per level



---

5. Objectives and Challenges

Main Goal:
To achieve a high score, players must understand and apply each grammar topic correctly. Success in the game depends not just on guessing, but on improving grammar skills through practice and learning.

Specific Goals:
- Develop a clear understanding of each grammar topic
- Apply correct grammar rules consistently
- Achieve high scores through accuracy and mastery
- Collect enough treats to unlock higher levels

Gameplay Challenges May Include:
- Time limits
- Perfect score targets
- Bonus streak mechanics

Consequence for Mistakes:
- Incorrect answers give fewer points and no treats
- Players are encouraged to review feedback, learn from mistakes, and retry levels to improve both score and understanding

---

6. Feedback System

Correct Answer: Provides positive visual and textual reinforcement

Incorrect Answer: Displays the correct answer with a clear explanation to support learning



---

7. Bonus Rewards (Optional)

Streak Bonus: +5 points for every 3 consecutive correct answers

Perfect Score Bonus: +2 treats for achieving 10/10 in a level



---

8. Achievement Recognition

Objective: Reward overall progress and dedication.

Players who collect 80–100 treats will receive a certificate of achievement


This system allows minor mistakes while still encouraging consistent effort and high performance.
