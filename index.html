Goal of the Online Web Application

The goal of the online web application is to help Grade 10 students improve their grammar skills in a simple, engaging, and effective way. It aims to address common difficulties in punctuation, capitalization, verb tense, subject–verb agreement, and sentence structure by providing interactive and easy-to-follow activities.

The application is designed to support the remedial grammar program by offering game-based exercises, guided lessons, and practice tasks that students can use independently. It also aims to help students apply grammar rules correctly, think critically through activities like error correction and sentence building, and improve their confidence in writing.

Overall, the web application seeks to make grammar learning more enjoyable while helping students develop clear, correct, and meaningful written communication.

About the Researchers

We, the researchers, are third-year English majors from Bulacan State University – Bustos Campus. As part of our academic endeavor, we are currently engaged in the creation and development of a grammar remedial syllabus designed to support learners in improving their grammatical competence.

In addition to this, we are collaboratively developing an online web application that aligns with and complements our remedial syllabus. This platform aims to provide accessible, interactive, and effective learning materials that will help students enhance their understanding and application of grammar in meaningful ways.

Through this project, we strive to integrate our knowledge, skills, and passion for teaching to create a practical and innovative tool that addresses the needs of learners in grammar development.
