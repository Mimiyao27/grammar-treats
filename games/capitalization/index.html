Capitalization

Definition:
Capitalization is the use of uppercase (capital) letters at the beginning of words or sentences.

Purpose:
It helps readers identify proper nouns, the start of sentences, and important words, making writing clearer and more organized.

Example:

Correct: Maria went to Manila on Monday.

Incorrect: maria went to manila on monday.
