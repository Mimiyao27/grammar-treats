Goal of the Online Web Application

The goal of the online web application is to help Grade 10 students improve their grammar skills in a simple, engaging, and effective way. It aims to address common difficulties in punctuation, capitalization, verb tense, subject–verb agreement, and sentence structure by providing interactive and easy-to-follow activities.

The application is designed to support the remedial grammar program by offering game-based exercises, guided lessons, and practice tasks that students can use independently. It also aims to help students apply grammar rules correctly, think critically through activities like error correction and sentence building, and improve their confidence in writing.

Overall, the web application seeks to make grammar learning more enjoyable while helping students develop clear, correct, and meaningful written communication.

About the Researchers

We, the researchers, are third-year English majors from Bulacan State University – Bustos Campus. As part of our academic endeavor, we are currently engaged in the creation and development of a grammar remedial syllabus designed to support learners in improving their grammatical competence.

In addition to this, we are collaboratively developing an online web application that aligns with and complements our remedial syllabus. This platform aims to provide accessible, interactive, and effective learning materials that will help students enhance their understanding and application of grammar in meaningful ways.

Through this project, we strive to integrate our knowledge, skills, and passion for teaching to create a practical and innovative tool that addresses the needs of learners in grammar development.

GAME MECHANICS

1. Scoring and Treat Collection

Players earn points for performance and treats as a progression currency. Points determine leaderboard ranking, while treats are used to unlock new levels.

Per Question Rewards:

  • ✅ Correct Answer: +10 points, +2 treats
  • ❌ Incorrect Answer: +2 points, 0 treats

2. Score Saving and Progress Tracking

After completing each level, players are required to manually save their score to record their progress. Only saved scores will:

  • Count toward the leaderboard
  • Be included in total points and treats
  • Be used for unlocking the next level

Note: Unsaved progress will not be recorded, encouraging responsibility and intentional progression.

3. Level Structure and Unlocking

The game consists of five levels, each focusing on a specific grammar skill:

Level 1:
Capitalization
Level 2:
Punctuation Marks
Level 3:
Grammar Proficiency
Level 4:
Arrange the Words
Level 5:
Sentence Correction

Treat Requirements to Unlock:

  • Unlock Level 2: 21 total treats
  • Unlock Level 3: 36 total treats
  • Unlock Level 4: 111 total treats
  • Unlock Level 5: 186 total treats

Treats are cumulative. Players who do not meet the required number must replay previous levels to earn more treats.

4. Performance Rankings

The system tracks and encourages competitive and consistent performance based on:

  • Total points earned
  • Total treats collected
  • Accuracy across all levels

5. Objectives and Challenges

Main Goal: Achieve a high score by understanding and applying each grammar topic. Success depends on master, not guessing.

  • Develop clear understanding of grammar topics
  • Apply rules consistently and achieve mastery

Challenges: Time limits, perfect score targets, and bonus streaks.

6. Feedback System

Correct: Positive visual and textual reinforcement.

Incorrect: Displays the correct answer with a clear explanation.
